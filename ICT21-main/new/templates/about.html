Visually identifying plant diseases is extremely inefficient, expensive, difficult and time consuming. Further, it requires the presence of a botanist which is not feasible in the Indian villages. Large scale manual observation with a very high accuracy and precision is close to impossible. Deep Learning technology can accurately detect presence of pests and diseases in the farms. Upon this Machine learning algorithm, CART can even predict accurately the chance of any disease and pest attacks in future. A normal human monitoring cannot accurately predict the amount and intensity of pests and diseases that have attacked a farm for spraying corrective medicines correctly and enough fertilisers /pesticides to eliminate the host.

Recognizing the problems of cultivation and dealing properly with them is a difficult task for the farmer and the agronomist. However, their importance for the productive and economic success of the agricultural enterprise is indisputable. Appropriate plant protection measures are crucial to address pathogens that cause disease and damage to plants. Appropriate and timely recognition of the agents (insects, mites, fungi, bacteria, nematodes), diagnosis of the problem and selection of active substances / products brings the desired results.
